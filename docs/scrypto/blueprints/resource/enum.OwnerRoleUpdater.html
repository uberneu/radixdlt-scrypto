Enum scrypto::blueprints::resource::OwnerRoleUpdater

pub enum OwnerRoleUpdater {
    None,
    Owner,
    Object,
}

Variants§

§

None

Owner is fixed and cannot be updated by anyone

§

Owner

Owner role may only be updated by the owner themself

§

Object

Owner role may be updated by the object containing the access rules. This is currently primarily used for Presecurified objects
